Historys biggest tank battle

The tank battle that ensued is renowned to be the largest to ever take place. This came about on the 12th of July as the Red Army had met the German tanks. This confrontation led to the Germans retreating. The outcome of the battle against the German advance had lefft the Soviets with 300-700 tanks permenantley or temporarily damaged whilst the Germans had lost 60-80 tanks.
