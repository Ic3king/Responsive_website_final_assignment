The Soviet Victory

"The Red Army and Navy and the whole Soviet people must fight for every inch of Soviet soil, fight to the last drop of blood for our towns and villages...onward, to victory!"
Joseph Stalin

The Battle of kursk resulted in the Red Armys victory. However the battle costed at least 500,000 lives, and 1, 614 tanks destroyed in the German Offensive However historians debate on the accuracy of the statistic given by the Soviet Union at the time.
